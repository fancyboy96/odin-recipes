Minestrone

Description

A hearty minestrone soup that will help you to live forever.

Ingredients

  • Macarone
  • 1 garlic bulb, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 sticks of celery finely chopped
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 carrots, finely chopped
  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 can broadbeans
  • 1 can lentils
  • 1 can tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated parmesan

Steps

  1. Saute onions, garlic, celery, carrots until sweated down.
  2. Add broadbeans and lentils
  3. Add tinned tomato
  4. Add chicken stock, water, bay leaves, salt and pepper and simmer for 10 minutes
  5. Add macarone, cook to preference and serve with grated parmesan.
