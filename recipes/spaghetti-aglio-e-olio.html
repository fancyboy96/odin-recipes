Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description

Spaghetti with garlic, oil, and parsley.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • 1 garlic bulb, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 lemon
  • 1/2 cup pasta water
  • 1 tablespoon chili
  • 1/2 cup parmesan
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in heavily salted water
  2. Heat olive oil in fry-pan
  3. with 5 minutes to go on the spaghetti fry the garlic for one minute
  4. Add chilli and parsley to fry-pan
  5. Add cooked spaghetti and pasta water
  6. Add lemon and parmesan
  7. Serve and eat immediately
