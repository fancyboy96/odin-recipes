Rapini & Potatoes

By David Rocco

Serves 4

Back to home

Description

Old school Italia is always best

Ingredients

  • 1 Rapini bunch, chopped
  • 3 Potatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1/3 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 Red chilli pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled chopped
  • 1 Lemon, zest and juice
  • 1 bunch basil, chopped
  • Salt QB
  • 500 mls Water

Steps

  1. In a pot or deep pan, add olive oil, garlic and red chilli pepper.
  2. Let sauté for a minute, and then add the potatoes.
  3. Add water, rapini and salt, and let cook for 20 minutes or until potatoes get soft and water has reduced by half.
  4. Smash 1/4 of the potatoes to make the dish a little creamy, zest a lemon and let rest for 20 minutes off heat.
  5. Before serving, add basil and a squeeze of one lemon.
  6. Serve with toasted Italian bread.
