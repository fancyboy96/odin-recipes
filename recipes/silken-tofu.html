Silken Tofu and Prawns

28.01.2024

By mizzerybell

Serves 4

Description

Just like you get at Yum Cha.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 3 silken tofu cubes, cut it half so theyâ€™re flat squares
  • 6 raw peeled prawns
  • cooking oil
  • sesame oil
  • soy sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • sugar

Steps

  1. Whisk egg and water 1:1 until smooth.
  2. Lay out silken tofu on baking dish, then lay prawns on top of each tofu square.
  3. Strain egg water mixture through a sieve on top of prawn/ tofu.
  4. Steam 8-10mins.
  5. In the mean time prepare the sauce by adding all sauce ingredients to a pan.
  6. When prawns/ eggs cooked through, pour sizzling sauce on top and serve.
